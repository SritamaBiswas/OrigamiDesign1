Facts of Animals and how to make them.

1.Camel

Interesting Facts About Camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels are adapted to hot climate.
  • Camels live in hot places like Rajasthan.
  • When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 150 litres in one go.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.


2.Pigeon

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals.
  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intellignet animals
  • In World War 2 pigeons saved human lives by carrying messages across enemy lines.
  • Pigeons were used as messengers.


3.Chameleon

Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  • Almost half of all the known species live in Madagaskar.
  • Chameleon vary a lot in terms of sizes.
  • Chameleons cannot regrow their tails like lizards.
  • Smaller chameleons have faster tongues.


4.Panda

Interesting Facts About Panda

  • They spend 10-15 hr time just eating.
  • They are good tree climber and swimmer.
  • They are omnivores.
  • Giant pandas average height is 1.2m and 1.5m,and weigh between 75kg and 135kg.


5.Cicada

Interesting Facts About cicada

  • Cicadas can't eat or bite because they lack jaws.
  • They use a straw-like appendage to suck sap from trees.
  • They have one of the longest insect lifespans.
